Features

After you log in you can choose a platform to aggregate photos from. At the moment, Youtube and Instagram are the two supported platforms. You will be redirected to their connect page so that we can aggregate your photos. You will have to select which photos you want to collect here or how many (the default value being the first 10 most recent).

After aggregating at least one photo, you will have multiple features unlocked. The first one is to visiualize important information related to the photo. If a card is clicked, you will see the number of likes and some comments from the photo. Also, the photo dimensions will be available. If you have more than one photo aggregated, you'll can swipe through them using arrows.

In the same menu presented above, the user has the ability to hide the comments and pop-up an editor. Using some sliders, you will can edit your photos applying filters while seeing the result in real-time. In the end, you can save the photo to your device.

If you wish to make a collage with your newly aggregated photos, you can! Just use Ctrl+LMB shortcut on one of the photos and it will be selected. After you select all the desired photos you wil have to click on a button to create a mosaic collage.

If you lost a photo due to the high number of aggregated media from your linked social media accounts, you can easily search it using our multi-cretiria search option. You can specify the platform it is from, its post date and other relevant information.

Last but not least, we'll try to implement the function to upload photos to your linked accounts. However, this feature is not available as for right now and it is yet to be announced officially. If you can find a dedicated page for uploading photos, you're in luck.
